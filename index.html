Step into the world of timeless elegance at Shyam Ji Farm House — a perfect blend of natural beauty, grand space, and refined hospitality, curated for your most treasured celebrations.

Spread across a beautifully landscaped 5,000 sq. ft. lawn and a well-appointed 3,500 sq. ft. air-conditioned hall, our venue comfortably accommodates gatherings of up to 1,000 to 1,200 guests. Whether you're planning a royal wedding, a festive reception, or a sophisticated corporate event, our versatile spaces offer the perfect canvas for your vision.

Our AC hall features seating arrangements for approximately 150 guests, ideal for ceremonies, dining, or VIP enclosures — while the expansive lawn area offers open-air elegance for floating guests, entertainment, and buffet setups. From day to night, the ambiance remains effortlessly luxurious.

To ensure a smooth experience for your guests, Shyam Ji Farm House provides two dedicated parking areas — spacious and secure, designed to accommodate cars and bikes with ease.

At Shyam Ji Farm House, we don’t just host events — we create unforgettable experiences marked by comfort, beauty, and seamless service.
